About Us

At Flare hacks we want students from around California to come together, form teams, and create a project over the duration of 24 hours. We are hoping to See many amzing projects the attendees come up with!

Here at Flare Hacks, we dont want to put any restrictions, build whatever your heart desires. Just make sure you have FUN!

Check out our Schedual and make sure to Join our Discord Server, which is where the hackathon is going to be hosted. Discord Make sure to Join our devpost as well. This is where you will be submitting your projects! Devpost
