About Us

At Flare hacks we want students from around the United States to come together, form teams, and create a project over the duration of 24 hours. We are hoping to See many amazing projects the attendees come up with!

Here at Flare Hacks, we dont want to put any restrictions, build whatever your heart desires. Just make sure you have FUN!

Check out our Schedule and make sure to Join our Discord Server, which is where the hackathon is going to be hosted. Please join our Discord and our Devpost.

EchoAR Workshop
By Echo AR

45 Min

The workshop will show how to quickly create AR/VR apps with no technical skills or coding required and scale existing apps by connecting them to the cloud. Workshop participants will learn how to create real-time cloud-connected augmented and virtual reality (AR/VR) apps using the echoAR platform. Participants are encouraged to bring a laptop and an Android phone, or just follow along the live demonstration. Platforms for experimentation include: Google ARCore, WebXR, Vuforia, Unity-based apps, and more.

Build a Resume Workshop
By Global Shapers Palo Alto

60 Min

GS Resume Review Workshop prepares those who are most impacted by COVID-19 to get back on their feet and into the workforce again. What will be happening? You will be paired with an experienced interviewer for a 10-20 minute online review session. Who are the reviewers? Palo Alto Global Shapers (https://www.paloaltoshapers.com) and experienced-interviewer volunteers. Who should sign up? Anyone, regardless of industry or experience-level, who would like a second pair of expert eyes over their resume.

FaQ

How much is Flare Hacks?
Nothing! Flare Hacks is absolutely FREE!
Whats the theme?
There is no specific theme, build whatever you desire.
What do I need to prepare for the hackathon?
Make sure you sign up on Typeform. After that make sure you join the Discord server.
Who can attend?
Anyone living in the United States and in middle school or high school can attend this hackathon.
What about teams?
Teams can be a maximum of 4 people. Our prizes are given out in 4 count, so this is why teams of more than 4 are highly not recommended.
